## **HOW TO USE THESE TOOLS**

1. **Fill them out regularly** - Don't wait until you remember. Set a time each week or month to update your tracking tools.

2. **Be honest** - These are for you. Don't sugarcoat, but also don't minimize your progress.

3. **Review them periodically** - Look back at where you started. See how far you've come.

4. **Celebrate progress** - Even small changes matter. Acknowledge them.

5. **Don't compare to others** - Your journey is yours. Track your own progress, not someone else's.

6. **Remember: Progress isn't linear** - You'll have good months and hard months. That's normal. Track it all.

---

*These tools help you see your progress when it's hard to see day-to-day. Use them. They're proof of your growth, evidence of your healing, and reminders of how far you've come.*
