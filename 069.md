## **ALPHABETICAL INDEX OF KEY TERMS**

### **A**

**Abandonment** - The deep emotional wound created when a child feels left, ignored, or emotionally neglected by caregivers. See: BASE.001 Section 1, 4.4; QUESTIONS.001 Section 6.1; Parables: "The Unheard Boy," "Why a Young Man Stops Caring About Others"

**Accountability** - Taking responsibility for your actions while maintaining self-compassion. See: BASE.001 Section 6; QUESTIONS.001 Section 6.6

**All-or-Nothing Thinking** - Seeing situations in black and white terms without recognizing middle ground. See: BASE.001 Section 7.2 (Cognitive Biases #1)

**Anger** - A secondary emotion that serves as protective armor over deeper wounds like hurt, fear, shame, or loneliness. See: BASE.001 Section 4.2, 5; QUESTIONS.001 Section 6.4; Parables: "The Fire Inside," "The Wounded Bear," "The Two Wolves by the River"

**Anxiety** - The emotional residue of unpredictability and abandonment, creating a nervous system that constantly scans for danger. See: BASE.001 Section 1, 4.5

**Authenticity** - Being genuine and true to yourself rather than wearing masks. See: QUESTIONS.001 Conclusion; Parables: "The Unfinished Mask," "The Seven Doors"

---

### **B**

**Blacksmith's Tongs** - The balance between control and release. See: BASE.001 Section 7.4 (Healing Concepts #4); Parable: "The Blacksmith's Tongs"

**Boundaries** - Healthy limits that protect you while allowing connection. See: BASE.001 Section 3; QUESTIONS.001 Section 6.3; Parables: "The Open Hand," "The Spear with Two Points"

**Broken Drum** - The erratic emotional rhythm that creates chaos in relationships. See: BASE.001 Section 7.3 (Defensive Patterns #4); Parable: "The Broken Drum"

---

### **C**

**Catastrophizing** - Automatically assuming the worst possible outcome will occur. See: BASE.001 Section 7.2 (Cognitive Biases #2)

**Character Forging** - The understanding that character is forged through proper shaping, not just endurance. See: BASE.001 Section 7.4 (Healing Concepts #11); Parable: "The Anvil and the Sword"

**Chained Giant** - Learned helplessness and the belief that you're powerless when you're no longer helpless. See: BASE.001 Section 4.6, 7.2 (Cognitive Biases #11); Parable: "The Chained Giant"

**Closed Fist** - The defensive posture of staying closed, guarded, and ready to strike. See: BASE.001 Section 7.3 (Defensive Patterns #1); Parable: "The Open Hand"

**Control** - The illusion of safety through trying to control things you can't control. See: BASE.001 Section 6.5; QUESTIONS.001 Section 6.5; Parables: "The Blacksmith's Tongs," "The Silent Stone"

---

### **D**

**Despair** - A quiet emotional collapse that occurs when hope goes silent. See: BASE.001 Section 4.3

**Discomfort vs. Danger Confusion** - The inability to distinguish between actual threat and emotional discomfort. See: BASE.001 Section 7.2 (Cognitive Biases #12); Parable: "The Warrior Who Chased Every Shadow"

**Disconnection (Emotional)** - The learned strategy of withdrawing from emotional connection to protect yourself. See: BASE.001 Section 7.1 (Emotions #17); Parable: "Why a Young Man Stops Caring About Others"

**Dragon** - Your accumulated unhealed wounds, protective patterns, and survival strategies. Not your enemy, but a guardian that needs understanding. See: BASE.001 Section 7.4 (Healing Concepts #1); Parables: "The Real Nature of the Dragon," "What It Means to Face the Dragon," "The Dragon of Wounded Pride"

---

### **E**

**Echoing Cave** - The principle that what you shout into the world returns to you in echoes. See: BASE.001 Section 7.4 (Healing Concepts #14); Parable: "The Echoing Cave"

**Emotional Reasoning** - Believing that feelings are facts and using emotions as evidence for truth. See: BASE.001 Section 7.2 (Cognitive Biases #3)

**Emotional Suppression** - The act of blocking or denying emotions rather than processing them. See: BASE.001 Section 7.1 (Emotions #20); Parable: "The Warrior Who Could Not Cry"

**Empty Lamp** - The state of giving from depletion rather than abundance. See: BASE.001 Section 7.3 (Defensive Patterns #5); Parable: "The Lamp with No Oil"

**Entitlement (Emotional)** - The unconscious belief that the world should compensate for childhood wounds. See: BASE.001 Section 7.1 (Emotions #5); Parables: "Respect: The Gateway to the Dragon," "How Respect Became a Path to Violence"

**Expectation as Reality Creation** - The belief that your expectations create your perceived reality. See: BASE.001 Section 7.2 (Cognitive Biases #13); Parable: "The Echoing Cave"

---

### **F**

**Facing the Dragon** - The process of claiming ownership of everything you once fled from. See: BASE.001 Section 7.4 (Healing Concepts #10); Parable: "What It Means to Face the Dragon"

**Fear** - Beyond physical danger, emotional fear includes terror of rejection, being unseen, misunderstood. See: BASE.001 Section 4.5, 5.1; QUESTIONS.001 Section 6.4

**Final Bow** - The act of bowing to your truth, accepting your totality with humility. See: BASE.001 Section 7.4 (Healing Concepts #9); Parable: "The Final Bow Before Battle"

**Forgiveness** - Letting go of the weight of the past while maintaining accountability. See: QUESTIONS.001 Section 6.7

---

### **G**

**Grief** - The natural response to loss that often goes unprocessed when related to childhood wounds. See: BASE.001 Section 7.1 (Emotions #7, #22)

**Grounding Techniques** - Methods to bring yourself back to the present moment when overwhelmed. See: CRISIS_RESOURCES.md; QUICK_REFERENCE_GUIDE.md

---

### **H**

**Haunted Blade** - The understanding that your tools carry the imprint of how you've used them. See: BASE.001 Section 7.4 (Healing Concepts #13); Parable: "The Haunted Blade"

**Helplessness** - The feeling of powerlessness that occurs when someone is forced into situations beyond their control. See: BASE.001 Section 4.6, 7.2 (Cognitive Biases #11); Parable: "The Chained Giant"

**Hope** - The fear of believing things can change. See: BASE.001 Section 4.3; QUESTIONS.001 Section 6.8

**Humility** - The ultimate armor against self-deception, involving honest acceptance of your totality. See: BASE.001 Section 7.1 (Emotions #21); Parable: "The Final Bow Before Battle"

**Hurt** - The primary emotional pain that occurs when something you value is damaged, threatened, or taken away. See: BASE.001 Section 7.1 (Emotions #23)

---

### **I**

**Insecurity** - The deep-seated feeling of inadequacy, uncertainty, or lack of confidence in your own worth. See: BASE.001 Section 5.6, 7.1 (Emotions #29); Parables: "Respect: The Gateway to the Dragon," "The Test of the Six Mirrors"

**Internal Dialogue** - The voice in your head that shapes your reality. See: BASE.001 Section 6; QUESTIONS.001 (all sections)

**Internal Helmet** - The mental defenses you must build to protect your mind from self-destructive beliefs. See: BASE.001 Section 7.4 (Healing Concepts #5); Parable: "The Shattered Helmet"

---

### **L**

**Learned Helplessness** - The belief that you're powerless to change your situation, formed when you were actually powerless as a child. See: BASE.001 Section 7.2 (Cognitive Biases #11); Parable: "The Chained Giant"

**Lion Who Feared His Roar** - The pattern of fearing your own power and intensity. See: BASE.001 Section 7.4 (Healing Concepts #12); Parable: "The Lion Who Feared His Roar"

**Loneliness** - The deep ache of feeling unseen, misunderstood, or disconnected from others. See: BASE.001 Section 4.7, 7.1 (Emotions #9)

---

### **M**

**Mask of Invulnerability** - The false self you show the world to hide fear, shame, and vulnerability. See: BASE.001 Section 7.2 (Cognitive Biases #15); Parable: "The Unfinished Mask"

**Masterless Horse** - Power without direction becomes destruction. See: BASE.001 Section 7.2 (Cognitive Biases #14), 7.4 (Healing Concepts #12); Parable: "The Masterless Horse"

**Mind Reading** - Assuming you know what others are thinking or feeling without evidence. See: BASE.001 Section 7.2 (Cognitive Biases #4)

---

### **N**

**Numbness** - Emotional shutdown that occurs when feelings become too overwhelming to process. See: BASE.001 Section 7.1 (Emotions #16); Parable: "Why a Young Man Stops Caring About Others"

---

### **O**

**Open Hand** - Opening instead of closing, receiving instead of demanding. See: BASE.001 Section 7.3 (Defensive Patterns #1); Parable: "The Open Hand"

---

### **P**

**Passion** - The intense emotional energy and drive that comes from caring deeply about something. See: BASE.001 Section 7.1 (Emotions #25); Parable: "The Masterless Horse"

**People-Pleasing** - A survival strategy disguised as kindness, where someone sacrifices their own needs to earn love. See: BASE.001 Section 1, 7.1 (Emotions #10); Parable: "The Lamp with No Oil"

**Perfectionism** - Shame disguised as high standards, where mistakes are interpreted as proof of unworthiness. See: BASE.001 Section 7.1 (Emotions #19); QUESTIONS.001 Section 6.5

**Personalization** - Taking responsibility for things outside your control or interpreting neutral events as being about you personally. See: BASE.001 Section 7.2 (Cognitive Biases #5)

**Power Without Mastery** - The dangerous state of having raw emotional intensity without direction, purpose, or discipline. See: BASE.001 Section 7.2 (Cognitive Biases #14); Parable: "The Masterless Horse"

**Pride (Wounded Pride)** - A brittle shield that hides rather than protects the heart. See: BASE.001 Section 7.1 (Emotions #15); Parable: "The Dragon of Wounded Pride"

**Projection** - Seeing your past emotional injuries in present situations. See: BASE.001 Section 1, 7.1 (Emotions #11)

---

### **R**

**Reactive Arrow** - The pattern of firing emotional arrows at every perceived threat. See: BASE.001 Section 7.3 (Defensive Patterns #3); Parable: "The Empty Quiver"

**Rejection Sensitivity** - Hypervigilance to signs of rejection or abandonment. See: BASE.001 Section 7.2 (Cognitive Biases #6); QUESTIONS.001 Section 6.1

**Resentment** - Accumulated anger and disappointment that builds when someone gives more than they receive. See: BASE.001 Section 2, 7.1 (Emotions #12); Parable: "The Lamp with No Oil"

**Respect** - Real respect is quiet, built in silence through choices made when no one is watching. See: BASE.001 Section 5.6; Parables: "Respect: The Gateway to the Dragon," "How Respect Became a Path to Violence"

**Rigidity** - The stubborn refusal to adapt, listen, learn, or feel. See: BASE.001 Section 7.1 (Emotions #18), 7.4 (Healing Concepts #15); Parable: "The Silent Stone"

---

### **S**

**Scarcity Mindset** - The belief that love, attention, or resources are limited and must be competed for. See: BASE.001 Section 7.2 (Cognitive Biases #7)

**Self-Fulfilling Prophecy** - Acting in ways that make your fears come true. See: BASE.001 Section 7.2 (Cognitive Biases #8); Parable: "The Echoing Cave"

**Self-Worth** - The internal recognition of your inherent value as a human being. See: BASE.001 Section 7.1 (Emotions #14); QUESTIONS.001 Section 6.2; Parables: "The Grandfather Speaks of Worth," "The Test of the Six Mirrors"

**Seven Doors** - The seven ways a warrior defends himself, only one of which leads to freedom. See: BASE.001 Section 7.3 (Defensive Patterns #2); Parable: "The Seven Doors"

**Shame** - The toxic belief that something is fundamentally wrong with you as a person. See: BASE.001 Section 7.1 (Emotions #13); QUESTIONS.001 Section 6.2

**Silent Stone** - The pattern of rigid resistance to change. See: BASE.001 Section 7.4 (Healing Concepts #15); Parable: "The Silent Stone"

**Six Mirrors** - The different aspects of yourself you must face. See: BASE.001 Section 7.4 (Healing Concepts #7); Parable: "The Test of the Six Mirrors"

**Survival Mode Thinking** - Operating from childhood survival strategies even when they're no longer needed. See: BASE.001 Section 7.2 (Cognitive Biases #9)

**Suspicion** - The defensive state of distrust and wariness that develops when you've been betrayed repeatedly. See: BASE.001 Section 7.1 (Emotions #24); Parable: "The Two Wolves by the River"

---

### **T**

**Terror** - The intense, overwhelming fear that goes beyond ordinary anxiety. See: BASE.001 Section 7.1 (Emotions #28); Parable: "The Test of the Six Mirrors"

**Threads of Fate** - The beliefs you've inherited and internalized that weave the tapestry of your life. See: BASE.001 Section 7.4 (Healing Concepts #8); Parable: "The Threads of Fate"

**Three Rivers** - The balance between thought, feeling, and action that creates wholeness. See: BASE.001 Section 7.4 (Healing Concepts #3); Parable: "The Three Rivers"

**Thornling Tree** - The defensive hardness that grows when you receive no nourishment. See: BASE.001 Section 7.3 (Defensive Patterns #7); Parable: "The Thornling Tree"

**Two Wolves** - The two impulses within you, reactive rage and reflective wisdom. See: BASE.001 Section 7.4 (Healing Concepts #2); Parable: "The Two Wolves by the River"

---

### **U**

**Unseen Wound** - The emotional injury that you pretend doesn't exist. See: BASE.001 Section 7.3 (Defensive Patterns #8); Parable: "The Unseen Wound"

---

### **V**

**Victim Mentality** - Believing that you have no power to change your circumstances. See: BASE.001 Section 7.2 (Cognitive Biases #10)

**Vulnerability** - The courage to show your true self, including your fears and needs. See: QUESTIONS.001 Section 6.4; Parables: "The Warrior Who Could Not Cry," "The Open Hand"

---

### **W**

**Walking Backward Pattern** - The habit of living in defense of old wounds, always facing the past. See: BASE.001 Section 7.3 (Defensive Patterns #6); Parable: "The Warrior Who Walked Backward"

**Worth (Self-Worth)** - The internal recognition of your inherent value as a human being. See: BASE.001 Section 7.1 (Emotions #14); QUESTIONS.001 Section 6.2; Parables: "The Grandfather Speaks of Worth," "The Test of the Six Mirrors"

**Wounded Bear** - The understanding that reactive aggression often stems from unacknowledged pain. See: BASE.001 Section 7.4 (Healing Concepts #6); Parable: "The Wounded Bear"
